Welcome to Hampi

Places to visit in Hampi:-

  1. Virupaksha Temple - Ancient, sacred Lord Shiva temple
  2. Vittala Temple - Iconic stone chariot, musical pillars
  3. Hampi Bazaar - Ruins, local market, cultural experience
  4. Matanga Hill - Best sunrise, panoramic views
  5. Lotus Mahal - Beautiful Indo-Islamic architectural marvel
  6. Elephant Stables - Historic royal elephants' resting place
  7. Queen's Bath - Royal bathing, Indo-Islamic architecture
  8. Hippie Island - Relaxed vibes, scenic riverfront
  9. Badavilinga Temple - Huge monolithic Shiva lingam
  10. Riverside Ruins - Carved rocks, temple remnants, history

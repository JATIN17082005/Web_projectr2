Welcome to Darjeeling

Places to Visit in Darjeeling:-

  1. Tiger Hill - Stunning sunrise, Kanchenjunga view
  2. Batasia Loop - Scenic railway, war memorial
  3. Darjeeling Himalayan Railway - Toy train, heritage steam ride
  4. Peace Pagoda - Buddhist shrine, serene surroundings
  5. Rock Garden - Waterfalls, terraced garden beauty
  6. Padmaja Naidu Zoo - Red panda, Himalayan wildlife sanctuary
  7. Happy Valley Tea Estate - Tea plantation, factory tour experience
  8. Observatory Hill - Religious site, panoramic mountain views
  9. Ghoom Monastery - Ancient monastery, spiritual ambiance
  10. Nightingale Park - Lush greenery, relaxing open space

Welcome to Jaipur

Places to Visit in Jaipur:-

  1. Amber Fort - Majestic fort, stunning hilltop views
  2. Hawa Mahal - Iconic palace, intricate window design
  3. City Palace - Royal residence, museum and artifacts
  4. Jantar Mantar - Astronomical observatory, UNESCO heritage site
  5. Nahargarh Fort - Scenic fort, panoramic city views
  6. Jaigarh Fort - Massive fort, historic cannon display
  7. Albert Hall Museum - Ancient artifacts, architectural beauty showcase
  8. Jal Mahal - Floating palace, picturesque water surroundings
  9. Birla Mandir - White marble temple, peaceful ambiance
  10. Galtaji Temple - Ancient temple, sacred water springs

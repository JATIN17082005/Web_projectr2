Welcome to Agra

Places to Visit in Agra:-

  1. Taj Mahal - Iconic marble mausoleum, eternal love
  2. Agra Fort - Mughal architecture, historic UNESCO site
  3. Fatehpur Sikri - Grand palace, Akbar’s former capital
  4. Mehtab Bagh - Sunset view, Taj Mahal backdrop
  5. Itmad-ud-Daulah's Tomb - Baby Taj, intricate marble craftsmanship
  6. Jama Masjid - Mughal-era mosque, architectural beauty
  7. Chini Ka Rauza - Persian-style tomb, colorful tile work
  8. Akbar’s Tomb - Majestic tomb, Mughal architectural masterpiece
  9. Guru Ka Tal - Historic Sikh pilgrimage, peaceful surroundings
  10. Keetham Lake - Scenic lake, birdwatching paradise

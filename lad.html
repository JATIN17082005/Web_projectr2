Welcome to Leh-Ladakh

Places to visit in Ladakh-Leh:-

  1. Pangong Lake – The stunning high-altitude lake that changes colors from blue to green.
  2. Nubra Valley – Famous for sand dunes, Bactrian camels, and breathtaking landscapes.
  3. Khardung La Pass – One of the world’s highest motorable roads at 18,380 ft.
  4. Magnetic Hill – A gravity-defying road where vehicles appear to move uphill on their own.
  5. Tso Moriri Lake – A beautiful, secluded lake surrounded by mountains and wildlife.
  6. Hemis Monastery – The largest and wealthiest monastery in Ladakh, known for the Hemis Festival.
  7. Shanti Stupa – A peaceful white-domed stupa offering panoramic views of Leh.
  8. Leh Palace – A historic palace resembling Tibet’s Potala Palace, offering stunning views of Leh town.
  9. Thiksey Monastery – A magnificent Buddhist monastery with a large statue of Maitreya Buddha.
  10. Zanskar Valley – Known for its rugged terrain, frozen river trek (Chadar Trek), and serene beauty.

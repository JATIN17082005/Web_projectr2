Welcome to Munnar

Places to visit in Munnar:-

  1. Tea Gardens - Lush green tea plantations
  2. Eravikulam National Park - Nilgiri tahr, breathtaking landscapes
  3. Mattupetty Dam - Scenic lake, speed boating
  4. Echo Point - Natural echoes, picturesque views
  5. Top Station - Stunning panoramic valley views
  6. Attukal Waterfalls - Gushing falls, trekking trails
  7. Pothamedu Viewpoint - Rolling hills, misty atmosphere
  8. Kundala Lake - Pedal boating, peaceful surroundings
  9. Chinnar Wildlife Sanctuary - Wildlife safari, diverse flora
  10. Blossom Park - Flowers, cycling, nature walks

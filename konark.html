Welcome to Konark

Places to visit in Konark:-

  1. Sun Temple - Architectural marvel, UNESCO heritage site
  2. Konark Beach - Serene beach, beautiful sunrise view
  3. ASI Museum - Ancient sculptures, historical artifacts displayed
  4. Ramachandi Temple - Sacred, dedicated to Goddess Ramachandi
  5. Kuruma - Buddhist site, archaeological excavation spot
  6. Chandrabhaga Beach - Tranquil vibes, golden sand stretch
  7. Pipili - Colorful applique handicrafts shopping
  8. Balukhand Wildlife Sanctuary - Deer, blackbucks, scenic nature trails
  9. Navagraha Temple - Nine planets, religious significance
  10. Archaeological Site Museum - Preserved relics, historical sculptures exhibit
